## Key Insights

- In 2009, the Technology category recorded the lowest overall sales and had comparatively fewer sub-category sales.  However, within this category, the Texas state generated the highest sales performance.
- In 2010, California recorded the highest sales in the Office Furniture category, and the Good Delivery rate was 72.50%.
- We observed that the Technology category experienced a decrease in the number of orders each year. However, it still generated the highest sales and highest profit among all categories, while maintaining comparatively lower discount rates than other categories.
- In 2011, the Illinois state recorded the highest product sales in the Office Supplies category.
- We observed that the Office Supplies category achieved the highest sales in November during 2011 and 2012. In addition, the highest sales were recorded in October 2009 and December 2010.
- The Furniture category consistently recorded the lowest sales performance across all years.

### Recommendations

- Increase investment in the Technology category due to its high profitability and strong sales performance.
- Improve promotional and pricing strategies for the Furniture category to boost sales.
- Focus marketing campaigns on high-performing states such as Texas, California, and Illinois.
- Prepare inventory and promotional offers ahead of peak sales months, especially October, November, and December.
- Maintain controlled discount strategies since lower discounts still produced strong profit in the Technology category.
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior further to improve future sales forecasting and business decision-making.
